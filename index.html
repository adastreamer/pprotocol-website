What is Product Protocol

Product Protocol is a opensource protocol for crowdfunding/crowdlending campaigns based on digital assets issuing, integration with all business processes, funds management and financial operations.

Product Protocol is focused on creating a platform that allows an entrepreneur to tokenize their assets and raise funds for scaling. A universal token for the tokenization of any assets allows you to instantly create a digital asset and a decentralized market will enable people from all over the world to purchase it.

Q/A

Product Protocol
What is Product Protocol? What is your aim?

Product Protocol is an opensource protocol for crowdfunding/crowdlending campaigns based on digital assets issuing, integration with all business processes, funds management and financial operations.

Our aim is not only to tokenize businesses, but also to create value, bring customers and companies together guaranteeing profitability for both sides.

How are you different from your competitors?

We see our goal in revolutionizing the relationship between businesses and customers, improve process and benefits of tokenization for companies, as well as make investing in tokenized projects more profitable for investors. On our blockchain assets desk, users do not just invest in projects by buying their tokens, but also receive services, products, and various bonuses.

How can I use PPO token? Why do I need it?

After the launch of blockchain assets desk our partnered businesses will regularly publish offers and lots. Without having PPO tokens on your balance you cannot participate in voting and buy lots.

In which countries are your offices located?

Our company representatives are located in Germany and Korea.

Businesses of what size are you mainly focused on?

We are mostly focused on medium and small businesses. However we are ready to consider all offers and will be glad to see your company on our platform.

How can businesses join your project?

In order to join PProtocol as a business, go to https://pprotocol.io, in Protocol section press “Connect your business” and fill out the form. We will shortly contact you back.

IEO
What is an IEO?

An Initial Exchange Offering is a token sale hold on an exchange. IEO works on different cryptocurrency exchanges depending on how it gets planned. The exchanges create a platform where the pool of developers can mint and sell the projects to crypto enthusiasts and investors!

What currencies can I buy PPO with?

During the Pre-IEO we accept BTC and ETH.

What is the funds allocation after the IEO?

Funds allocation is the following:
45% — Technology development
20% — Marketing and ecosystem
20% — Reserves
10% — Team and salaries
5% — Legal and administrative

What is the price of 1 PPO token?

For now you can buy 1 PPO for 0,001 $ additionally getting special bonuses depending on the sum of your investment.

Is the PPO token based on the Ethereum blockchain?

No, we have own blockchain solution.

Is there a minimum/maximum size of purchase?

No

Do you have softcap/hardcap?

Our softcap is estimated at 5,000,000$. However we are on the negotiations stage with huge investors and therefore IEO might be closed before reaching it. So hurry up to get the best offer!

Is there any assurance that your token won't lose its price in the future?

After the IEO exchanges block certain amount of collected funds to maintain the price level. Also they support via own marketing activities.
